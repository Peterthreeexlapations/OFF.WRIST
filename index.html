OUR STORY

YOUR PIECE.
YOUR COLOR.

OFF.WRIST is an independent aftermarket accessories brand built around a single idea — collectible pocket-style watch heads deserve to be worn. The AP x Swatch Royal Oak collab shipped in a sealed pocket box: a watch head, no strap, and no real way to put it on your wrist without permanently modifying the piece. We engineered around that limitation.

Our flagship product is the OFF.WRIST Conversion Case — a precision-fit matte shell with an integrated sport-watch strap that houses the watch head securely without altering it. Tool-free fit. Zero modifications to the watch itself. The result is a wearable wristwatch silhouette in seconds: pocket to wrist.

Every case is manufactured in premium-spec facilities from a lightweight composite, then coated in a matte finish and paired with an integrated strap finished to the exact same hand-feel as the case. Case, links, and strap unify into one cohesive piece. Sixteen colors in the current drop — from blacked-out daily drivers to high-vis hype tones — built for everyday wearability and collector-grade fit.

OFF.WRIST sits at the intersection of collectible culture, modern industrial design, and wearable luxury-inspired accessories. Designed for collectors. Made for the culture. Off the pocket. On your wrist.
